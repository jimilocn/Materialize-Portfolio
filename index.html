Vietnamese

Jimi was born in Saigon, Vietnam. He is heavily influenced by the innate culture, textures, flavors, and warm personalities that runs through his blood.

American

Jimi hails from the lower peninsula of Mitten State. he grew up in a small farming community near Grand Rapids, and he was nutured in an environment that balances morales, motivation, drive, grit, passion, creativity, and efficacy. This generated a perfect storm internally to create a foundation for success.

The Arts

Jimi became well practiced in Flute, Clarinet, Saxophone, and Violin during his primary education. Throughout his years , he transitioned his efforts from music composition and arrangement, to material arts, graphic design, and even movement and dance choreography.

Education

Jimi is a University of Michigan Alumni in the School of Music Theatre and Dance, and he is currently at the Northwestern University studying software development and design.

Jimi Today

Today, Jimi works as a Chiropractic technician at The Chiro One Wellness Center of Andersonville by day, an overly enthusiastic coder by night at Northwestern University in Evanston, and a professional contemporary dancer during the weekend. He is excited for his trajectory forward into the world of technology and computer sciences.
